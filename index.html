FAQ

Questions, answered

Can't find what you're looking for? Get in touch.

Is Recall really free?
Yes — the free plan includes spaced repetition, up to 3 decks, and basic analytics. Pro is optional and unlocks unlimited decks, advanced analytics, and exam mode.
When does early access start?
We're rolling out invites now. Apply above and you'll get an email when your spot is ready — usually within a few days.
Which exam boards do you support?
AQA, OCR, Edexcel, CIE, IB, AP, and SAT. Each deck is tagged with the board and specification it covers.
Can I import my own cards?
Absolutely. Paste from a spreadsheet, upload a CSV, or use our Anki import — anything you already have can come with you.
Does it work on my phone?
Yep. Recall is a web app, so it works on any device with a browser. Decks sync between phone and desktop automatically.
How is my data handled?
Your study data is yours. We never sell it, never share it with third parties, and you can export or delete it whenever you want.

Controls

  • Space ↑ or Click/Tap — Jump (tap for short hop, hold for full jump)
  • One hit kills — spikes and gaps restart the level
  • Land on platforms and blocks to cross obstacles

Level Builder

  • Left click / drag — place object   Right click — erase / toggle gap
  • Scroll — pan left/right   Test Play to run your level instantly
  • Publish to Site — share with the community (add a thumbnail image!)

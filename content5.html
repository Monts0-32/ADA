README.md

Controls

  • Space ↑ or Click / Tap — Jump (hold for longer jump)
  • Avoid spikes and gaps — one hit and you restart from the beginning
  • Land on platforms and blocks to navigate high obstacles

Level Builder

  • Left click / drag — Place selected object
  • Right click — Erase object or toggle a ground gap
  • Scroll wheel — Pan the level left / right
  • Press Test Play to instantly run your level
  • Press Publish to Site to share with the community

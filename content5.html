README.md

Controls

  • Space ↑ or Click — Jump (hold for longer jump)
  • Avoid spikes and gaps — one hit and you restart
  • Land on platforms and blocks to navigate

Level Builder

  • Left click — Place selected object
  • Right click — Erase object / toggle ground gap
  • Scroll — Pan the level left/right
  • Press Test Play to instantly run your level
  • Levels are saved to localStorage
